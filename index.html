On the Waves of Piracy

Open-Sourcing Traditional Culture, One Song at a Time (Using FastAPI)

Samuel Petroviƒç

Motivation

  • Songbooks are awesome, but paper is a terrible medium
  • Difficult to obtain
  • Songbooks are databases anyway
  • We want to query
  • Poor accessibility

Songbook webapp

Requirements

  • Free access
  • Modern framework
  • Good filter/search
  • Custom collections
  • Accessibility

Songbook webapp

Implementation

  • Backend: FastAPI, Jinja2
  • Database: SQLmodel, postgres, alembic
  • Music: Lilypond
  • Frontend: Bootstrap, HTMX
